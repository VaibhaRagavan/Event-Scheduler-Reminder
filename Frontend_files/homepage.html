🥳 About Us

Welcome to WishPal – your personal celebration assistant! 🎉

We know how important it is to remember the special days of the people who matter most to you. That's why we created this platform – a place where you can easily store birthdays and anniversaries of your friends and family.

  • 📅 Add events like birthdays or anniversaries or any other events and reminders
  • 📬 We send a heartfelt wish on your behalf.
  • ✅ You’ll get a notification once the message has been delivered.

No more forgotten wishes. No more last-minute scrambles. Just thoughtful messages sent on time, every time.
Let us help you spread smiles and stay connected – effortlessly.
